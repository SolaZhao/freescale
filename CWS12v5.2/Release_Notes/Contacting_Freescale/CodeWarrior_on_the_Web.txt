WORLDWIDE SUPPORT

We are pleased to offer you a 24-hour-a-day, 365-day-per-year
information and support resource available on our World-Wide Web at

     http://www.freescale.com/support

With our web support, you can:
  - ask technical support, or registration questions
  - link to other developer resources
  - file bug reports or feature suggestions
  - download updates, patches, and new versions of tools
  - find user-contributed tips, and techniques
  - find out more about Freescale Developer Technology 
  - find online documentation, white papers and searchable knowledgebase
 
Worldwide help is available to anyone with an Internet connection. 
This includes users of America Online, other internet provider
who can use their online service's World-Wide Web browser.
